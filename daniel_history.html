History / About Us

Saint Paul Preparatory Seoul (SPPS) DECA Chapter was first founded by president Daniel Hong and co-president Steven Ryu in August 2019. DECA is a decent business club, which would be helpful for students who will major in business, finance, management, entrepreneurs, and marketing. Club activities will make students experience how market and business works. Through those experiences, we improve our skills with selling and managing the club. Also, we learn step by step how the market operates. Chapter is the official club in SPPS, and also the official chapter in DECA organization. All the members in chapter are all officially registered in DECA.
